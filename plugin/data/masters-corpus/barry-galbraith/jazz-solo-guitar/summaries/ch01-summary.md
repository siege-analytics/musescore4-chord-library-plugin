# Chapter 1 — 42 Chord Melody Arrangements for Solo Guitar

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch01.md`._

Chapter 1 contains no load-bearing prose passages — it is composed entirely of notated chord-melody arrangements. The book is a collection of 42 jazz-standard arrangements for solo guitar by Barry Galbraith (e.g., A Certain Smile, Alone Together, Autumn in New York, As Time Goes By, Have You Met Miss Jones?, How About You, I Cover the Waterfront, and many others), presented as fully voiced guitar notation without instructional commentary. The pedagogical content is encoded in the voicings, voice-leading, and chord substitutions Galbraith chose for each tune, not in explanatory prose.
