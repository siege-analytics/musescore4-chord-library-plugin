## Quartal chord definition

> (aka 4th chords) are built by stacking 4th intervals above the lowest
> note of the chord.

**Significance (p. 59).** This is the foundational definition of quartal chords — the structural rule that distinguishes them from tertian harmony.

## Quartal chords: musical context

> Quartal chords sound more open and modern compared to other chord types and can
> be used over any jazz standard in your comping and solos.

**Significance (p. 59).** This gives the player a concrete application rule — quartal chords are not context-specific but universally applicable over jazz standards in both comping and soloing.
