# Chapter 1 — Study 1 - Blues For Alice

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch01.md`._

Chapter 1 contains no load-bearing prose passages — composed entirely of notated patterns and exercises for the Blues for Alice study. As the book’s opening Study, it establishes the pedagogical template: learning jazz vocabulary through melodic phrases notated over a standard tune’s chord changes. By anchoring patterns to the blues form, this chapter demonstrates how to build fluency with idiomatic lines in a musically coherent context rather than in isolation.
