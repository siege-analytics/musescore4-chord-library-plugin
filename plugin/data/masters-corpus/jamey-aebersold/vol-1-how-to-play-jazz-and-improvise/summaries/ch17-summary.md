# Chapter 17 — Song Lists & Discography

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch17.md`._

This chapter presents a comprehensive 13-step procedure for internalizing jazz tunes, placing listening and ear training at the center of jazz musicianship. The master's characteristic moves—emphasizing the 3rd's and 7th's as guide tones, anchoring improvisation to the original melody, and mapping harmonic knowledge onto the instrument itself—reflect a philosophy that prioritizes ear development and melodic internalization over purely technical study. The chapter introduces no major new terminology but reinforces load-bearing concepts like guide tones, chord progression, and the distinction between melody-based and harmony-based improvisation, all organized around a single governing principle: that the ear, not notation or theory, is the best teacher, and that recordings contain all the answers needed for learning jazz.
