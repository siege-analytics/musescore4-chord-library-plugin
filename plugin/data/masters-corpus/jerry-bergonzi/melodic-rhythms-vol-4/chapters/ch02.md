## Rhythm displacement concept

> After playing each of the one-bar rhythms through Tune 2 the next step is to alter the placement
> of each rhythm. For example, you can play each rhythm one beat early, or two beats early, or one
> beat late, and so on. These following exercises will help you to hear rhythms independent of bar
> lines and will help you to develop “phrasing over the bar

**Significance (p. 16).** Defines the core technique of rhythmic displacement and names its musical goal hearing across bar lines.

## Half-beat displacement effect

> Notice that by displacing a rhythm 1/2 of a beat, or 1 1/2 beats, or 2 1/2 beats, you create entirely
> different sounding rhythms at times.

**Significance (p. 16).** Rule teaching that off-half-beat displacements produce perceptually distinct rhythms.
