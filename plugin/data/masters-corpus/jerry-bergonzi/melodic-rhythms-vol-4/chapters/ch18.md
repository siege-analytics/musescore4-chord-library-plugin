## eighth notes in the pocket

> One way to improve your feel or placement of eighth notes is to notice how your eighth notes fit
> with the rhythm section which is often playing off of the triplet. In jazz playing, feeling the triplet
> can help you to feel the groove of the rhythm section and help you to place your eighth notes “in
> the pocket.”

**Significance (p. 77).** Defines pocket placement via triplet pulse.

## eighth notes vs. triplet feel

> This doesn’t mean that your eighth notes should be played as triplets like in this
> example, but rather that your eighth notes should be able to coexist with the triplet feel

**Significance (p. 77).** Coexistence with triplet feel, not literal triplet playing.
