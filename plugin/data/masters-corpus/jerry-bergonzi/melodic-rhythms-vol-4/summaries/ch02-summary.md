# Chapter 2 — 22 Rhythms

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch02.md`._

Chapter 2 teaches rhythmic displacement: playing the 22 rhythms from Chapter 1 at different metrical positions relative to the bar line. The master's characteristic move is pedagogical: moving students from bar-line-dependent hearing toward phrase-level rhythmic independence by systematically shifting each rhythm early or late (by whole beats or half-beats). The load-bearing concept is that displacement by half-beat intervals (1/2 beat, 1 1/2 beats, 2 1/2 beats) creates perceptually distinct rhythmic patterns, revealing that rhythm is a relational property dependent on its position within the measure. This chapter introduces the terminology of phrasing over the bar line, the ability to hear rhythmic coherence across metrical boundaries.
