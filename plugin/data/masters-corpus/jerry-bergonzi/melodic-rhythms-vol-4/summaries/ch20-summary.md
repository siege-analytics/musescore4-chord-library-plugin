# Chapter 20 — In the pocket

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch20.md`._

Chapter 20 covers in the pocket as a rhythmic refinement skill, teaching gear-change practice: spontaneous switching between eighth-note and triplet feels through random rather than programmed application for transferable fluency. Load-bearing concepts include adapting melodic lines to varying time zones (generalizing gear-shifting beyond chord exercises) and the eighth-note to triplet conversion technique, allowing any scale or intervallic sequence to transform into triplet patterns. A refinement move is triplet adaptation through first-notes omission, enabling mid-phrase access to the triplet version of a line.
