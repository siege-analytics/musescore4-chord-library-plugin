# Chapter 21 — Half-time quarter-note triplet feel

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch21.md`._

Chapter 21 covers half-time quarter-note triplet feel through rhythmic practice centered on time-zone isolation. The method: isolate and master a single time zone using Tune 9 (Twinkle Twinkle), systematically advance through additional zones, then combine multiple zones by varying between them. Load-bearing concepts are time zones as rhythmic practice vehicles and composition as benchmark for mastery: students compose their own scale and intervallic sequences. This reflects the characteristic pedagogical move of building technique through self-generated material, where ability to compose variations demonstrates mastery of the underlying feel.
