# Chapter 25 — Switching gears

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch25.md`._

Chapter 25 (Switching gears) introduces the distinction between downbeat and upbeat playing as separate technical exercises, establishing downbeats and upbeats as load-bearing terminology for rhythmic control. The chapter isolates each element first then progresses to mixing downbeats and upbeats on a single tune, integrating these isolated skills into fluid quarter-note soloing. This reflects a characteristic pedagogical move of building technique from component parts into cohesive musical phrases.
