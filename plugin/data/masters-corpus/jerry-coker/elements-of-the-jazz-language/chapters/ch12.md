## CESH harmonic contexts

> The supposed harmonic setting for a CESH to take place is a minor chord of long
> duration. However, the examples from recorded solos will reveal that improvisers will
> also use.CESH on II-7 chords, V7 chords (the minor CESH will actually be built on the
> fifth of the V7, as in G - ona C7), or the II-V in combination.

**Significance (p. 74).** Rule extending CESH application beyond minor chords to II-7, V7, and II-V.
