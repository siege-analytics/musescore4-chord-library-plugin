## Dorian Mode parent key rule

> The student must remember to relate each Dorian Mede to its parent key before attempting to ap-
> ply the numerical formulas for the formation of the four types of minor chords and before attempting
> to play the scale (dorian mode) of these chords.

**Significance (p. 63).** This is the method's core procedural rule for all minor chord work: always derive the Dorian mode from its parent major key before applying any formula.

## Minor seventh chord formula

> Tones No. 1-3-5-7 of the B Dorian Mode form the B Minor Seventh Chord.

**Significance (p. 67).** Defines the numerical formula for building a minor seventh chord from the Dorian mode.

## Minor ninth chord formula

> Tones No. 1-3-5-7-9 of the B Dorian Mode form the B Minor Ninth Chord.

**Significance (p. 67).** Extends the chord-building formula to the ninth, showing how adding one scale degree transforms a minor seventh into a minor ninth chord.
