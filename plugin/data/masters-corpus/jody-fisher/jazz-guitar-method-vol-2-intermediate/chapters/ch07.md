## Turnaround definition

> A turnaround is a two- or four-measure section at a first ending, or at the end of a tune. A turnaround creates a smooth harmonic transition back to the first measure.

**Significance (p. 86).** Foundational definition of turnaround.

## Turnaround usage contexts

> You will find turnarounds in most jazz tunes. Generally, the guitarist can choose to play any one of the many kinds of turnarounds possible and it's up to the other players to react. For variety's sake, it pays to know a lot of different turnarounds. Using a variety of turnarounds will create unexpected twists and turns in an arrangement. They also make good intros and endings.

**Significance (p. 86).** Performance rule: guitarist leads turnaround choices, value of variety.
