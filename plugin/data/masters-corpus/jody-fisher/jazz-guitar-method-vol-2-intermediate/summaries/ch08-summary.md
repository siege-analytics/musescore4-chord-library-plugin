# Chapter 8 — Chapter 8

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch08.md`._

This chapter teaches how to improvise through turnarounds by learning and memorizing transposable licks that create melodically smooth transitions back to tune beginnings, and it presents two synthesis pieces—a chord solo and a single-note solo, both loosely based on "Misty"—that reinforce the book's A-lesson chord-melody concepts and B-lesson improvisation techniques respectively. The load-bearing terminology introduced includes "turnarounds" as harmonic passages requiring specific improvisation strategies, and "licks" as reusable melodic phrases to be memorized and transposed across different chord progressions.
