## Expanding picking hand approach

> Often times you learn chords and then strum and pluck them without altering the
> picking.
>
> This is leaving a lot of room to explore in your comping by expanding on your
> picking hand approach to these chords.

**Significance (p. 9).** States the core methodological principle of the chapter — that the picking hand, not just chord shapes, is the key variable in advanced comping.
