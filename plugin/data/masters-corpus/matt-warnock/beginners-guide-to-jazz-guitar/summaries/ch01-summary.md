# Chapter 1 — Beginner's Guide to Jazz Guitar Intro

_Distillation; backed by `chapters/ch01.md`._

This chapter is introductory rather than instructional, establishing the conceptual roadmap and pedagogical framework for the entire jazz guitar method. The course elements overview identifies the core curriculum components—Chord Shapes, Chord Rhythms, Picking Patterns, Chromatic Chords, Major Scales, Chromatic Ornaments, and Mixing Chords and Scales—all presented within the foundational ii V I progression. The recommended study sequence introduces the load-bearing methodology of the text: watch video lessons alongside the PDF, review the initial chapter, practice each section until comfortable, then advance systematically through subsequent chapters. This protocol establishes the characteristic pedagogical approach of the method—iterative reinforcement combined with deliberate pacing—that students will apply throughout their progression through the material.
