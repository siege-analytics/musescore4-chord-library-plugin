## Symmetric Harmony — parallel motion rule

> Let me bring out this fact. Symetric Harmony, as we are applying it, moves from one
> point to another. What goes on between these two points is of no consequence. Harmony
> is parallel either up.or down and you can have as many as 12 tonics (Exercise 42).

**Significance (p. 24).** Governing rule for symmetric harmony: only start and end points matter, with strictly parallel motion and up to 12 tonics.
