## Cadence Break definition

> the Cadence Break occurs at the end of a musical
> thought. Looking through any popular sheet music, you will see that at certain designated
> points the music relaxes for one or two bars and then either repeats itself or progresses
> into another musical line. These points are known as the Cadence Breaks

**Significance (p. 36).** Foundational definition of the Cadence Break concept.

## Harmonizing the Cadence Break

> Semetric Cycles, Peda] Point and other types of original chord progressions may be em-
> ployed at these points. The musical thought has ended. Any new chord progressions that
> may be added give new color and originality.

**Significance (p. 36).** Rule for how to fill a Cadence Break — Symmetric Cycles, Pedal Point, or new progressions.

## Pedal Point definition

> A Pedal Point is a sustained or pedal note held or sustained for several measures
> while a variety of chords are introduced. The most popular note in Pedal Point is the fifth
> of the scale.

**Significance (p. 37).** Core definition of Pedal Point including the rule that the fifth of the scale is the canonical pedal.
