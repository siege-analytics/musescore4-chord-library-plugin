## Chord-on-chord convergence rule

> The main chords in tunes may be converged on or embellished
> with other chords that may precede or lead up to them. Whatever
> chord you are leading up to (main chord) is or can be usually pre-
> ceded by a five chord with a tritone. This interval participates
> in this five or dominant-seventh chord as well as another seventh
> chord a flatted 5th from its root.

**Significance (p. 66).** Foundational rule of chord-on-chord convergence.
