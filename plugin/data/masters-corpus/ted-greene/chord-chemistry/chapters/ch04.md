## Chord formula definition

> Every chord has a certain combination of tones that distinguishes it from every other chord. These
> tones may be derived from the major scale with the same root as the chord name.

**Significance (p. 10).** Core principle that chord construction derives from the major scale.

## Triad definition

> The
> particular group of tones that is used for the construction of a particular kind of chord is called the

**Significance (p. 10).** Defines 'formula' and 'triad' terms used throughout.

> When tones are
