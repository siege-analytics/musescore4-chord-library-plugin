## self-teaching altered tone rules

> Example: You might say to yourself, “769+ chords are great on VI7;” this is a
> “rule” you might remember.. Or you might go further and generalize in a similar manner to what was said before:
> “VI7 sounds good with any altered tone when the VI7 is used in smooth connection with the chords that precede or
> follow it.”
